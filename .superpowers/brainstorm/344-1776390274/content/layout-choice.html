My default recommendation is A — drawer on desktop, bottom sheet on mobile. It's the pattern Fresha / Google Calendar / Notion use, and it lets coaches keep the calendar visible while they tweak a lesson. But your screenshot looks like a mobile/dark interface, so C could feel more native if you primarily use this on your phone.
