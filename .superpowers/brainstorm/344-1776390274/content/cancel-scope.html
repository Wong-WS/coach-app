This lesson — writes a classException with type: 'cancelled'. Past lessonLogs untouched. Wallet history preserved.
This and future — sets endDate on the booking to the day before. Series stops. Past lessonLogs untouched.
All lessons — deletes the booking + all its classExceptions + all its lessonLogs. Requires a second confirm. Wallet charges refunded.
