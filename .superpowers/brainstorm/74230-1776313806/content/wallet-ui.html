Wallet Management UI

Where does the wallet live in the dashboard?

A

Wallet tab inside Students page

Students page gets two tabs: "Students" and "Wallets". Keeps payment and students together. On a student card, you see which wallet they're linked to.

B

Separate Wallets page in sidebar

New nav item "Wallets" between Students and Locations. Dedicated page for all wallet management. Student page just shows a wallet badge per student.

C

Replace Income page with Payments page

Current Income page becomes a "Payments" page with tabs: "Overview" (income stats), "Wallets" (manage wallets), "History" (all transactions). Consolidates all money stuff.
